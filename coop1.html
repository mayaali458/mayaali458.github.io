Small Drinking Water Systems Management

As a small drinking water systems assistant at PWBS, I gained valuable hands-on experience in the operation and maintenance of water treatment systems.

My primary responsibilities included:

Engineered and executed a comprehensive water quality monitoring project: Conducted over 300 tests across five municipalities, identified critical control points, and managed public health risks.

Conducted site inspections and evaluated Engineering Evaluation Reports (EER): Collaborated with health inspectors and engineers to ensure regulatory compliance, addressing all critical points and requirements.

Organized and executed microbiological and lead sampling: Performed water quality tests, including bacteria and lead testing, following Ontario Regulation 170, ensuring the safety of drinking water in schools and public facilities.

Developed and documented procedures for water quality management: Maintained compliance with Ontario regulations through meticulous record-keeping and data cross-referencing.

Maintaining accurate daily system logs to track flows, chlorine residuals, turbidity readings, and other parameters. I compiled these into monthly summary reports.

Conducting routine maintenance tasks such as filter backwashing, chlorinator maintenance, and UV lamp replacements to maintain proper operation.

Applied engineering principles in practical scenarios: Utilized knowledge of fluid dynamics, chemical processes, and environmental science to enhance water treatment and distribution systems.

Coordinated product and material orders for water quality management projects: Managed inventory, placed orders, and oversaw the submission of paperwork upon product arrival to streamline accounting operations.

Through professional collaborations and oversight of multifaceted fieldwork, I strengthened critical thinking, communication, and project leadership skills essential for tackling intricate engineering initiatives. My time at PWBS provided an excellent opportunity to learn about real-world drinking water system operations and strengthened my passion for providing safe, sustainable solutions.
